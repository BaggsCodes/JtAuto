Services we offer

Sale of both new and used tires

We offer a wide range of tires, including both new and used ones, at highly competitive prices. Our services cover vehicles of all sizes, from cars to 18-wheelers. If you're looking for new wheels, visit us today. If we don't have what you need today, we can have it ordered for you by tomorrow.

Mechanical work

We take pride in offering a comprehensive range of services beyond tire repair. Our team of experts is equipped to tackle various mechanical issues that may be hindering your ability to drive. Schedule an appointment with us and we'll be more than happy to assist in resolving any vehicle-related concerns you may have.

Air check

We understand the importance of hard work and family, and to us, our customers are just like family. That's why we provide a complimentary air check service during every visit. When your vehicle is in our shop, you can trust that it's in good hands.
